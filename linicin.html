Teaching children about head lice

How do you teach children what it means to have head lice when you can not see them? You create a fun and engaging game that the kids can play while they are being treated for the lice.

▶ Check it out
"I am super proud of this game. We made the entire thing in less than a month - the sounds where made over night! But the result is so good. The gameplay, the design, the sounds, everything."
ANDREAS BØDKER
CEO, ABTION

THE DESIGN

No words allowed

The game should be for everyone. Therefore all words was banned from the very beginning. This made great demands on the design, but the solution is a game that is so intuitive that even the youngst children immediately understands what it is about.

THE SOUNDS

Parent friendly sounds

We were careful not make the sounds too enervating - it is stressful enough that your kid has lice. The result is a soft sound universe that is both engaging for the child and cheerfull - almost meditative - for the parents.

"Ultimately, we found that interactions with the game had to be very lightweight, so keeping content digestible within a few seconds was essential."
Jesper Sørensen
Game developer
