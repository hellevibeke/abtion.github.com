Know your headache

Are you aware of the factors that can help trigger a migraine attack? If you know what you need to keep away from, it may be easier for you to prevent an attack. Know your headache is an app that helps you track your headache. With the app you can record how bad your pain is, how long your headache last, and see if where if it was triggered by specific foods or situations.

THE DESIGN

No text allowed

The game should be for everyone. Therefore all text was banned from the very beginning. This made great demands on the design, but the solution is a game that is so intuitive that even the youngst children immediately understands what it is about.
